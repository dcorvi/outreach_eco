Our Story

Outreach Eco is a small family business based in Massachusetts USA.

We focus in creating a better work and healthier life environment by producing blue light blocking glasses for the digital world we live in.​

Our products are designed and created by specialists with a love for the environment and providing a better quality of life for you! So, you can be sure our products are perfect for anyone searching for better quality living and comfort.

Outreach Eco is dedicated to fulfilling the following:

  • An affordable and comfortable life solution for people
  • Creation and design of fashionable eyeglasses for everyone
  • Provide high performance and quality for our customers’ products
  • The convenience of ordering eyeglasses online
  • To provide excellent customer support

We are dedicated to providing not only a safe shopping experience, but truly care about your quality of living and comfort!
